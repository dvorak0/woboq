28/**
29 * Compute the minimum spanning tree (MST) using Kruskal's algorithm
30 * @param fg Factor graph
31 * @param weights Weights of the edges/factors in the factor graph
32 * @return Edge/factor indices spanning the MST
33 * @note Only binary factors are considered while constructing the spanning tree
34 * @note The indices of 'weights' should match the indices of the edges in the factor graph
35 */
